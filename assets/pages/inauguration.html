BYTE BEGIN Inauguration Event - Highlights

Welcome Speech

The BYTE BEGIN Inauguration event commenced with an inspiring speech by our esteemed HoD, Anu Varghese, who welcomed everyone to the grand launch of our department's core activities for the academic year 2025. She emphasized the importance of technology in shaping the future and encouraged students to embrace innovation and explore new possibilities in the field of computer science.

Inaugural Address by Guest Speaker

Our honored guest, the renowned tech expert, delivered an inaugural address on the significance of computer science in solving global challenges. The speaker's insights about the latest advancements in AI, cybersecurity, and the role of students in driving innovation were both motivational and enlightening.

Launch of BYTE BEGIN Programs

Following the speeches, the BYTE BEGIN team, led by Riswana (Secretary), Shiyas (Joint Secretary), and Fathima K A (Core Team Member), officially launched a series of programs and initiatives for the year. These include workshops, hackathons, and competitions that will engage and empower students throughout the semester.

Networking Session

One of the key highlights of the inauguration was the networking session, where students, faculty, and industry professionals interacted and exchanged ideas. This provided a fantastic opportunity for students to connect with mentors and explore career opportunities in the tech industry.

Program Showcases

During the event, BYTE BEGIN showcased some of its ongoing projects and upcoming events. These included a sneak peek into the upcoming hackathons, tech talks, and other exciting initiatives aimed at nurturing the next generation of tech leaders.
