Cyber Day 2025 - Highlights

Event Overview

Held on February 15, 2025, Cyber Day 2025 was an exciting celebration of technology, knowledge, and innovation. The event aimed to raise awareness about cybersecurity and digital literacy through engaging sessions and hands-on experiences.

Tech Talks

Industry professionals and guest speakers conducted insightful tech talks covering topics like ethical hacking, AI security, and digital privacy. Students had the opportunity to interact and ask questions directly to experts.

Hands-On Workshops

Interactive workshops allowed students to practice network scanning, understand vulnerabilities, and experiment with cybersecurity tools. Teams collaborated to complete challenges and strengthen their skills.

Cybersecurity Awareness Campaign

A highlight of the day was the student-led awareness campaign promoting cyber hygiene, password safety, phishing awareness, and data protection. Posters, interactive quizzes, and street plays helped spread the message.

Live Phishing Demo

One of the most engaging parts of Cyber Day was the Live Phishing Demo, where students learned how phishing attacks are crafted and delivered in real-world scenarios. A simulated phishing email was sent to volunteers, showing how easily one can be tricked — and how to identify the red flags.

This demo emphasized the importance of vigilance while browsing and checking emails. Attendees were shown the anatomy of a phishing page, along with techniques used to bypass security filters.
